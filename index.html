Multiverse Maven, Singular Passion

I’m Adhi, a Digital Designer from Indonesia. 🇮🇩 I started in architecture, but now pixels and code are my playground. I thrive at the intersection of design and tech. Oh, and did I mention I’m also weaving sustainable fashion dreams with my brand, REPUTASEE?

More About Me

Focus on Business Management, Unlocking Cultural Nuances, Digital Craftsmanship, & Conscious Style-Weaving

Architect of Business

As a seasoned Managing Director, I orchestrate the symphony of our family business. My blueprint extends beyond bricks and mortar; it encompasses strategy, trust-building, and financial harmony. I read reports like an architect scrutinizes blueprints, ensuring our company’s survival and growth. And yes, managing payroll? That’s my structural engineering feat.

Language Explorer

Embarking on a linguistic adventure, I've embraced the following languages:

  • English (Upper-Intermediate): With a strong grasp of grammar and composition, English is the medium through which I express my digital creativity and articulate complex concepts.
  • Korean (Intermediate): After more than four years of intensive self-learning, I can navigate conversations, enjoy music, and watch variety shows without subtitles, delving deeper into the language's rich cultural tapestry.

Design & Code Artisan

My UX/UI design journey extends beyond just aesthetics. While I use no-code builders like Framer and Webflow to create stunning layouts, coding (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) allows me to push the boundaries of interactivity and design. It’s about taking control, enhancing the user experience, and turning creative ideas into functional reality.

Fashion Admirer

While I may not be the one designing haute couture, my passion for sustainable fashion is the heartbeat of REPUTASEE. This brand I’m bringing to life in Indonesia is currently in the R&D phase, threading together style and sustainability. Keep your eyes peeled on our Instagram – we’re excited to share our journey and updates with you!
